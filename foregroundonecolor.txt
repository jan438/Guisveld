Don't say you're dumb, this operation takes a few steps that aren't the most intuitive.

Here's how I would do it:

Choose the Color Select tool (Shift+O) and click somewhere transparent to select all of your transparency
Invert your selection with Ctrl+I (or use the top toolbar for Select -> Invert)
Click your foreground color swatch to find the color you want for your single-color-filled object. (If the color you want is somewhere in your image, you can use your Color Picker tool (O) to click and get the color)
Select your Bucket tool (Shift+B) and ensure that, in your Tool Options section, you have the Affected Area set to "Fill whole selection"
Click in the object you want to fill
